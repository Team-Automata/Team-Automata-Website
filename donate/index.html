We are Team Automata, a small self-funded robotics team based in Arizona. For over 7 years, we have been competing in various robotics competitions, volunteering at robotics events, and, more recently, hosting robotics camps and mentoring younger teams. Our team has been very successful, winning numerous awards at the local, state, regional and international level. For three years in a row we have been the Arizona State VEX Robotics Championship Excellence Award winner. The Excellence Award is the highest honor given at a VEX competition. In addition, last year we were the recipient of the 2014 VEX Robotics World Championship Innovate Award for our unique Shifting-X Drive. Please browse our website for more information on our projects and accomplishments.
