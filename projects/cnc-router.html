DIY CNC Router

My CNC Router is based off of plans from Momus CNC. I modified the plans to extend the work area of the machine and to allow it to cut dovetail joints on the ends of boards. The router is fully enclosed and has a 15"x28"x5" work area.

We showed the machine at the Southwest Maker Fest and the 10th Annual Maker Faire Bay Area.
