Created to compete in VEX Toss Up

The Warthog was designed for scoring quickly and consistently. The Warthog's double tank-tread intake design allowed for fast and easy Buckyball pickup. In order to pick up large balls, the Warthog used a large flip-out scoop. A multitude of sensors aided in both driver mode and autonomous mode.
