Created to compete in VRC Sack Attack

The Pelican was designed for scoring quickly and consistently in both driver and autonomous modes. The arm design allowed for either side of the robot to act as the front, increasing scoring speed by eliminating the need to turn around. A high bucket gear ratio allowed the robot to descore, while a tilting plate raised on linear slides enabled the robot to score in the high goal. Mecanum wheel drive allowed for movement in all directions. A multitude of sensors were used in both driver mode and autonomous mode.
