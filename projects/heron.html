Created to compete in VRC Gateway

The Heron was designed for fast, precise scoring. The polycarbonate claw had a 3 object capacity. A tilting four bar lift allowed the Heron to score in all heights of goals, and reach into the opponent's isolation 20" goal. Two servo powered tabs at the base of the claw allowed version 2 to quickly stack multiple objects. Sensors on the robot included potentiometers for arm presets, shaft encoders on the drive wheels, and jumpers to select the autonomous mode. A retracting finger on the claw allowed for descoring of the top object in a goal.
