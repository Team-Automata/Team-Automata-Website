Winner of the 2014 Dallas Regional Founders Award

Created to compete in BEST Blade Runner

The C.C.R.A.B.B. (Cave Creek Robotics Automated Blade Builder) was designed for quickly transporting and installing wind turbine components. The robot featured a linear lift with two claws to carry up to three turbine blades at once. It also had a hook to tow a turbine nacelle and a device to safely move prairie chickens out of the robot's way. The drivebase used three custom omni-wheels built out of polypropylene, piano wire, and aluminum rod to provide holonomic motion.
